Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Apli.ai?

Apli.ai is Indian born company founded at Global Entrepreneurship Boot camp at Indonesia which works towards helping Students to improve on their personality and to grab better opportunities with Artificial Intelligence at IIT Bombay

2. I want to post a complaint/comment on the website's functionality. Where can I do that from?

If you want to lodge/register a complaint about any of the site's disfunctionality or abnormality then we would love to help you out. Its simple. Log into your account then under the feedback tab lodge/register your complaint with relevant screenshots(optional).We will contact you back as soon as possible.

3. How can I add another user to my team?

If you want to invite a user to your team then log into your respective account then under the Team tan there is a button with the name "Add Member". Click on that button and enter the relevant details. An email would be sent to inform the person that he has been invited to a team with the name of the person and the role alloted to him.He can signup further to be added to your team.

4. I have forgot my password . What should I do ?

If you have forgot your password then do not worry because we have got you covered with the option of resetting your password. To reset your password simply click on the "Forgot Password?" under the Login button. An email with a password reset link would be sent to Your registered email-id. You can then click on the link and follow the procedure to successfully reset your password.

5. What is the Timeline for ?

The timeline as the name represents shows your recent activities and actions that you have done. From adding a user to a team to removing a user from the team , editing the user,its role,deleting the user from the team , to posting the jobs and any other kind of changes that you make will be shown on the timeline.

6. Where can I can get answers to my other Questions?

If you want to ask something more or something you couldn't fathom then feel free to ping us using ReachUs. We will try to get back to you as soon as possible.
